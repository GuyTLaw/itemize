Frequently Asked Customer Support Questions

Software Cost How much will the software cost?

There is a free version for any user that creates an account but there will be a paid version for advanced features that costs $15 a month.


Account Registration How do I register my account?

You can use a valid email log in to create an account with us to access the website features. We promise not to share your data in any way with third parties.


Deals Are there any bulk deals for large companies?

We work with companies on a one-on-one basis to determine the best product fit, and work with our suppliers to provide the lowest cost experience that we can.


General Support I'm having issue with the software, who can I contact?

You can contact our onsite support team at any time at the following email address: ItemizeCompany@Gmail.com


Troubleshooting What if I accidentally delete all my data?

We store your data in the cloud. While we cannot directly view your data, we can retrieve previous versions of what you have saved in the event of accidental deletion or other event.
